Professional Profile

An executive-standard background built on clinical excellence.

Dr. Mansour combines academic rigor, global training, and a design-led approach to dentistry. His work focuses on functional harmony, aesthetic balance, and treatments that feel as exceptional as they look.

Biography

With nearly two decades of practice, Dr. Tariq Mansour has built a reputation for transforming complex cases into elegant, long-lasting results. His clinic serves patients seeking cosmetic enhancement, restorative rehabilitation, and premium preventive care in a calm, highly private environment.

He has completed advanced continuing education in oral implantology, digital dentistry, porcelain veneers, full-mouth reconstruction, and occlusion-driven rehabilitation. His treatment planning is known for detail, transparency, and a personalized experience tailored to each patient’s goals.
